AI Smart SOS Emergency Response System

The AI Smart SOS Emergency Response System is an advanced safety feature integrated into our AI Smart Dashcam project to provide immediate assistance during accidents, driver unconsciousness, or dangerous road situations. The system continuously monitors the driver using AI, Computer Vision, OpenCV, and MediaPipe technologies by tracking eye movement, head position, driver attention, and vehicle behavior in real time.

When the AI detects a severe collision, unconscious driver condition, prolonged inactivity, unsafe driving pattern, or manual SOS trigger, the emergency mode activates automatically. The system instantly generates voice alerts, switches to SOS mode, and collects critical emergency data including live GPS location, driver status, incident time, and vehicle information.

After confirmation, emergency notifications containing the live location and accident details are automatically sent to family members, emergency contacts, ambulance services, police stations, or fleet management systems. This rapid alert mechanism helps reduce rescue response time and improves survival chances during emergencies.

The SOS system is designed using AI-powered monitoring, real-time processing, GPS integration, alert automation, and embedded system technologies such as Raspberry Pi, making it affordable, scalable, and suitable for cars, trucks, taxis, buses, and smart transportation systems.

By combining intelligent monitoring, automated emergency communication, and real-time tracking, the AI Smart SOS System transforms a normal dashcam into a life-saving smart safety assistant that enhances road safety and supports faster emergency response operations.
